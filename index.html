Delegation Framework

The 4 modes of working with AI

Use this as a reference when planning tasks in your project.

⚡

AI-Led / Automate

Routine, templated, or high-volume tasks. AI handles end-to-end with light human review. Examples: formatting, summarising, translating, first drafts of structured content.

🤝

Collaborate / Augment

AI generates, human judges and refines. Best for tasks requiring domain expertise + AI scale. Examples: research synthesis, strategy drafts, content that needs your voice.

🧠

Human-Led / Assisted

Human does the core work; AI provides support, ideas, or checking. Examples: relationship decisions, novel creative work, tasks needing lived experience or accountability.

🚫

Human Only / Skip AI

AI would subtract value. Authentic personal voice, ethical decisions, high-stakes judgment calls, or tasks where AI errors carry unacceptable risk.
